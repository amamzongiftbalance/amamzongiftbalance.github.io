How to Check Your Amazon Gift Card Balance Online

Checking your Amazon gift card balance is simple and secure. Whether you're shopping or just curious about your remaining funds, follow these steps:

  • Go to Amazon.com and sign in.
  • Hover over “Accounts & Lists” and click on Gift Cards.
  • Your current Amazon balance will be displayed.

Check Amazon Gift Card Balance on iPhone & Android

Want to manage your balance on the go? The Amazon app makes it easy:

  • Open the app and tap the Menu (☰) icon.
  • Select “Your Account” > “Manage Gift Card Balance.”
  • You’ll see your gift card balance and options to reload, add, or redeem.

Add a Gift Card to Your Amazon Balance

To add gift card to Amazon balance, simply:

  • Go to the “Gift Card” section in your account.
  • Click “Redeem a Gift Card”.
  • Enter your claim code and click “Apply to your balance.”

View Remaining Amazon Gift Card Balance Before Purchase

Wondering how much is left? During checkout:

  • Select your payment method.
  • Amazon will automatically show your remaining gift card balance.
  • If the Amazon gift card balance is too low, pay the rest using another method.

Does Amazon Gift Card Balance Expire?

No — Amazon gift card balances do not expire. Once added, the balance stays until it’s used.
